About Centro

At Centro, we revolutionize business operations through world-class BPO services and cutting-edge technology solutions. Focused on efficiency, scalability, and innovation, we help organizations streamline processes, enhance performance, and drive sustainable growth. With deep industry expertise, advanced technology, and an unwavering commitment to operational excellence, we deliver customized outsourcing solutions that elevate customer experiences, optimize workflows, and unlock business potential. Our approach is rooted in precision, accountability, and innovation, ensuring seamless integration with our clients' operations. More than just an outsourcing provider, we are a strategic partner—empowering businesses to adapt, grow, and lead in an ever-evolving market.

Centro History

A Journey of Growth
& Excellence

  • 2009

    Hesham Farag founded Centro in Fairfax, Virginia, with a vision to enhance customer experience.

  • 2010

    Centro expanded globally, launching its first major delivery site in the heart of Cairo, Egypt.

  • 2015

    Centro’s reach expanded to a new operational site in the heart of Manila, Philippines.

  • 2016

    A state-of-the-art facility was launched in Winchester, Virginia, to strengthen operational outreach.

  • 2021

    Centro's services are diversified to include datasphere, digital experience, and customer experience solutions.

  • 2022

    Centro launched a cutting-edge, dedicated CX center in El-Gouna, Red Sea Governate, Egypt.

  • 2023

    Centro's footprint expands to Dubai and Riyadh, reinforcing its commitment to global excellence.
